Directions

 

If coming by metro:

Get off at El Maadi.  If coming from Cairo you need to exit immediately, if coming from the South then cross the bridge before you exit the station. Turn left onto Street 9 and walk north for two blocks. Turn right on Street 77 and then take the second left on Street 11. MCS is on the left with a blue steel gate.

 

If coming by car:

  1. Once in Maadi ask directions for Midan Sawaris towards the area called Hadeyek El Maadi.
